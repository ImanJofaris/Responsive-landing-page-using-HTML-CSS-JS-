Refinance everything.

Take control of your finances with the
50/30/20 rule.

Needs (50%)

Allocate 50% for essential expenses like housing, food, utilities, and transportation to live within your means and cover your basics.

Wants (30%)

Allocate 30% for enjoyable things like dining out, hobbies, travel, and entertainment to treat yourself without overspending or neglecting savings.

Savings (20%)

Allocate 20% for long-term financial goals like retirement, emergency fund, or debt repayment to build a strong financial foundation for your future.
